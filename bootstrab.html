How to play basketball


Standing correctly:

When the player is with the ball, he must stand correctly to
protect it and be able to dribble, so that the bend
is done with the knees spaced to the width of the shoulders.

Ball dribbling:

This is done by bouncing the ball on the ground using
the tips of the fingers of the hand, not the palm of the palm.

The ball stays at the waist:

it must be kept low on the ground, as this helps in dribbling,and
also reduces the chance of the opponent to catch the ball and take it.

Head up:

Keep your head up and look at the perimeter rather than
looking directly at the ball while it's bouncing.
This helps monitor teammates, opponents and court boundaries,
as well as quick passes and dribbling.
